    8.To display go to urls.py where under url pattern add ...path('home/', views.Home, name='my-home').. this gives it the url pattern eg on the browser http://127.0.0.1:8000/home/...you added the /home/ in front of ...http://127.0.0.1:8000... to display index.html content
